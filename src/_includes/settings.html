Support & Information
Price data is graciously provided by CoinMarketCap. It's updated when you open the extension popover. It is limited to update every 60 seconds. If you open the popover multiple times within a 60 seconds timeframe it will use old data until those 60 seconds have passed.

If you'd like to leave feedback or report a bug, visit the extension repository.
